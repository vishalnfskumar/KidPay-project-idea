How It Works

Simple data flow for speed, privacy and security.

  1. KidPay Card — Makes payment & sends GPS data.
  2. Cloud Server — Secure ingestion, encryption & processing.
  3. Parent App — Real-time tracking, spending controls & alerts.
  4. Payment Gateway — UPI settlement & transaction flow.
